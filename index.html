About Me

From Educator and Engineer to Advocate for Progress

Educational Background:

  • PhD in Agriculture and Soil Engineering.
  • Master’s in Mechanical Engineering.

Professional Achievements:

  • Associate Professor at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology.
  • Developed models to maximize water resource efficiency in arid regions.
  • Innovated drought-resistant irrigation systems using modern technology.
  • Published groundbreaking studies on the untapped agricultural potential of semi-arid lands.

My life’s work has been about solving real problems with real solutions. As your leader, I will bring this same dedication and innovation to our region.

Agenda

  • Education: Increase funding for schools, integrate technology into classrooms, and offer scholarships for technical fields.
  • Economic Security: Promote entrepreneurship, create jobs through agricultural innovation, and establish financial literacy programs.
  • Climate and Environment: Advocate for afforestation, implement sustainable farming practices, and develop eco-friendly water management systems.

Ongoing Projects

  • Green Fields Initiative: Equipping farmers with modern tools and knowledge to improve yields while protecting the environment. Trained over 500 farmers in water conservation techniques.
  • Education for All: Introducing solar-powered classrooms and providing e-learning materials. Partnered with NGOs to distribute 1,000 tablets to underserved students.
  • Regional Drought Response Plan: Developing early-warning systems and drought-resistant irrigation models. Saved over 2,000 acres of farmland from desertification.

Plan for the Region

Fostering Education: Build more schools in underserved areas, partner with international organizations to fund vocational training programs, and offer free school meals to ensure children stay in school.

Ensuring Economic Security: Set up regional industrial parks to boost manufacturing and create jobs, facilitate access to low-interest loans for small business owners, and expand irrigation systems to increase agricultural output.

Championing the Environment: Launch community tree-planting initiatives with a goal to plant 1 million trees in 5 years, incentivize farmers to adopt sustainable farming methods, and develop clean energy programs, including wind and solar projects, for the community.
